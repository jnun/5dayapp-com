Money Flow & Escrow

We Never Touch Funds

Critical: Platform is not a custodian. All funds flow directly to qualified third-party escrow agent

ACH via Plaid

Investor authorizes ACH transfer from verified bank account ‚Üí Escrow account

Held in Escrow

Funds held until campaign closes or cancels; if canceled, funds returned automatically

Disbursement to Company

On successful close, escrow agent releases funds to company; we facilitate instructions only

Blockchain Record

Security & Compliance Architecture

Every transaction, document access, admin action logged immutably; queryable for FINRA inspections
