Our Moat: Security & Legal Compliance

This is the hardest part of the business and our single greatest competitive advantage. We are not just building a website; we are building a regulated financial institution.

Legal Mandate

FINRA Registration

Must register as a Funding Portal with the SEC and FINRA before facilitating any transactions. Our seed round directly funds the legal and operational costs of this process.

Regulation Compliance

Platform logic built to enforce Regulation Crowdfunding (Reg CF):

  • Enforcing investment limits for non-accredited investors
  • Mandating all required disclosures (Form C) for Founders
  • Managing all communications in a compliant manner

Security Mandate

User Verification

Mandatory KYC/AML checks on all users (Founders and Investors) to prevent fraud.

Fund Security

We will never hold client funds. All funds processed directly via integrated, qualified third-party escrow agent.

Data Security

All sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit. Rigorous access controls (2FA, role-based access) from day one.

Required Audits & Certifications

To build trust and ensure institutional readiness, we will pursue the following audits before our public launch (funded by the seed round):

🏛️

FINRA Compliance Audit

FINRA will audit our platform, processes, and personnel before granting our Funding Portal license. This is non-negotiable.

🛡️

External Penetration Test

Third-party security firm will conduct a full penetration test to identify and remediate vulnerabilities.

⭐

SOC 2 Type II Report

Gold standard for FinTech. Begin certification during MVP build to demonstrate commitment to security, availability, and confidentiality.

06

Product Roadmap: From Seed to Series A

Our seed round is focused on launching a secure, compliant, and functional core product. Our Series A will be for scaling and feature expansion.

The "Bootstrap" Version

Seed Round Goal - MVP

The MVP is a fully-compliant platform focused on one thing: executing a Reg CF raise from start to finish.

For Founders

Simple wizard to build a campaign page, upload legal documents, and track committed funds.

For Investors

Browse deals, review compliance documents (secure data room), e-sign subscription agreement, and transfer funds to escrow.

For Admin

Robust back-end to vet and approve campaigns, manage user KYC/AML status, and monitor all transactions for compliance.

The "Robust" Version

Series A Goal - Scale

Once the core model is proven, we will use future funding to build a market-leading platform.

Secondary Market

The #1 requested feature. A fully-compliant Alternative Trading System (ATS) for investors to trade their shares, providing crucial liquidity.

Mobile-First App

Dedicated native app (iOS/Android) for investors to discover, research, and manage their portfolios.

Advanced Investor Tools

Portfolio analytics, diversification wizards, and AI-driven deal recommendations.

Expansion

New investment types (real estate, collectibles) and support for other regulations (Reg D, Reg A+).
