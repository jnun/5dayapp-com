How We Build It: Architecture & Compliance

üë§

Investor Onboarding & KYC

Step 1: Express Interest

Investor signs up for campaign updates; automatically subscribed to email newsletter for ongoing raise communications

Step 2: Complete KYC

Plaid Identity verifies name, DOB, SSN, address; AML screening against OFAC/sanctions lists

Step 3: Calculate Investment Limit

System determines max investment allowed based on annual income or net worth per RegCF rules

Step 4: Legal Approval

Compliance team reviews and approves investor profile before investment access is granted

Result

Investor can now invest up to their legally allowed limit in any new opportunity

‚úçÔ∏è

Document Signing & Validation

Subscription Agreement

Auto-generated from template with investor details pre-filled

DocuSign E-Signature

Legally binding e-signature with IP address, timestamp, and certificate of completion

Document Storage

Encrypted PDFs stored in AWS S3 with versioning and audit logs; 7-year retention per SEC requirements

Compliance Trail

Every signature, view, and download logged in PostgreSQL for regulatory audits

üíµ

Money Flow & Escrow

Step 1: Investment Commitment

Investor commits funds; ACH transfer authorized via Plaid from verified bank account

Step 2: Funds to Escrow

Money flows directly to qualified third-party escrow/bank account (we never touch funds)

Step 3: Transaction Tracking

Platform tracks each investment to specific escrow account; blockchain record created for immutability

Step 4: Legal Approval for Disbursement

Company completes required compliance steps; legal counsel approves fund release conditions

Step 5: Release or Return

Campaign success: Escrow releases to company. Campaign canceled: Automatic return to investors

Security & Compliance Architecture

Data Encryption

AES-256 at rest (PostgreSQL TDE), TLS 1.3 in transit; PII encrypted at field level with separate key management

RegCF Automation

Platform enforces: $5M campaign max, investor limits based on income, mandatory Form C disclosures, 21-day review period

SOC 2 Type II

AWS infrastructure + application controls audited annually for security, availability, confidentiality

Audit Trails

Every transaction, document access, admin action logged immutably; queryable for FINRA inspections

Penetration Testing

Annual third-party pentest + bug bounty program; vulnerabilities remediated within 30 days

Result: A platform architected to meet FINRA Funding Portal requirements from day one‚Äînot bolted on later.
